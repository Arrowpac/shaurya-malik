Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan

VVM is India'a largest online science talent search exam conducted by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India.

    Objectives -

  • To provide hands-on training to students through workshops and other events.
  • To identify successful students at the State and National levels and felicitate them.

Vikram Sarabhai Science Foundation

The VSSF SPOT is an annual National Science Promotion Orientation Test conducted by the Vikram Sarabhai Science Foundation (VSSF) to identify and assist bright students with a strong aptitude for science.

    Objectives -

  • Build confidence and communication abilities in a world increasingly shaped by science and technology.
  • Create a dynamic, integrated research community by simplifying science and inspiring the next generation of innovators.

National Children's Science Congress

National Children's Science Congress (NCSC) is a nationwide Science Communication programme started in the year 1993. It is a programme of National Council for Science and Technology Communication (NCSTC), Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India.

    Objectives -

  • To create a forum for children aged 10–17, from both schools and out of school, to showcase creativity, innovation, and science-based solutions to local societal problems."
  • To relate science learning to the local physical and social environment, making science more relevant to everyday life.

KVS National Integration Camp (English Debate)

The KVS National Integration Camp, now largely integrated into the broader Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative, is an annual program by Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) designed to foster national integration and cultural understanding among students.

    Objectives -

  • Cultural Exchange - Students from different regions exchange ideas and knowledge about other states' cultures.
